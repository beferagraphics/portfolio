The Objective

In January 2018, my partner and I set our sights on starting our first small business. Creating REALBAD was a long and arduous journey that led me down paths in the design process that both frustrated me and pushed me to try even harder.

After many attempts at creating a brand image that reflected my values and humor, I created REALBAD, a brand on a mission to make personal authenticity cool to wear.

Our goal for REALBAD was to make the name alone be recognizable and relatable.

I created dozens of shirt designs for REALBAD, most of which made it onto our Shopify and Amazon Merch stores.

I also created advertisements for social media, mostly Facebook and Instagram, and got to see a bit of how Facebook works for businesses.

The Results

Though REALBAD didn't pan out like we wanted, it was still a great learning experience in the realms of both design and business management. I will be utilizing what I've learned from making REALBAD shirts and marketing the products to create new businesses in the near future.
